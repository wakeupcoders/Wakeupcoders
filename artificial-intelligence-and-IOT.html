Artificial Intelligence & Internet of Things

From N8N workflow automation to Ollama model deployments — production-ready AI & IoT solutions.


We combine AI models, automated workflows and IoT edge integrations to deliver scalable, secure and maintainable solutions for real-world problems.

N8N Workflow Automation

We build event-driven pipelines using N8N to automate data collection, preprocessing, model inference triggers, notifications and integrations with third-party services. N8N flows let you orchestrate complex processes without brittle glue code.

  • Data ingestion & transformation
  • Automated retraining pipelines
  • Webhooks, cron jobs and event triggers
  • Monitoring & alerting integrations

Ollama Model Deployment

We deploy and serve language and ML models using Ollama for fast, secure inference close to your application. Our deployments focus on reproducibility, latency optimisation, and secure access controls.

  • Containerised model hosting
  • Low-latency inference APIs
  • Model versioning and rollback
  • Secure auth & observability

IoT & Edge Integrations

From sensor connectivity to edge inference, we design IoT systems that gather reliable telemetry, apply local ML inference where needed and funnel important events into N8N-driven workflows and central dashboards.

  • Sensor onboarding & secure provisioning
  • Edge model inference & caching
  • MQTT/HTTP data pipelines
  • Telemetry storage & analytics
